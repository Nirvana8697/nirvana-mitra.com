NirvanaMitra

Assistant Professor of Economics & Public Policy
Indian School of Business, Hyderabad

My research spans sovereign debt and defaults, consumer finance, and the macroeconomic consequences of institutional design. I combine quantitative theory with empirical methods to understand how governments and households borrow, default, and recover.
